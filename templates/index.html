Refresh

A M2M solution to overuse of plastic bottles

The Problem

Discarded plastic drink bottles account for 38% of Australia’s total rubbish volume. Out of all bottles purchased, less than 40% of these are recycled - the other 60% become landfill or clog our waterways. These dumped bottles can take up to 1000 years to decompose - and with Australians spending over $500 million on bottled water every year, the number of discarded bottles is ever increasing. Keep Australia Beautiful recognises this waste as a significant program in Australia’s environmental make-up, and offers grants in collaboration with the Coca-Cola Foundation to people that want to ameliorate this issue.

Our Solution

Our solution is a communal vending machine that delivers a reusable water bottle, filled with chilled or sparkling water, to consumers. The machine uses an NFC reader system that allows customers to swipe a card, and collect a bottle for use over 48 hours. Refills at the machine are given free of charge, whilst sparkling water requires a fee per refill. If the customer has the bottle out of the machine for over 48 hours, they will incur a fine each day until the bottle is returned to the machine. When returned, the bottle will be cleaned and ready for pick-up by the next customer that uses the machine.
